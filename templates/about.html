About CloudBridge

CloudBridge began as a dissertation project for my final year at university. I was drawn to the challenges and potential of peer-to-peer cloud gaming and what it holds for the future of cloud gaming.

The aim of CloudBridge is to help foster and facilitate a thriving provider-client ecosystem within the peer-to-peer cloud gaming landscape, by developing a discovery platform that bridges providers and clients, hence the name, CloudBridge.

CloudBridge will be revolutionizing the world of cloud gaming by providing a platform where users can seamlessly connect with both cloud gaming providers and clients seeking enhanced gaming experiences.

Our mission is to break down barriers and make cloud gaming accessible to all, regardless of hardware limitations or geographical location.

Join us on this journey as we reshape the future of gaming together!
